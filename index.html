01 — Text & Typography

The Foundation of Communication

What is Multimedia?

Multimedia refers to the integration of multiple forms of content — text, audio, images, animation, and video — into a single interactive experience. Modern digital systems leverage these modalities to communicate complex ideas efficiently.

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." — Steve Jobs

Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) is the discipline that studies how people interact with computers and designs systems that are safe, efficient, and enjoyable to use. Every interface decision — from button placement to colour contrast — affects usability.

Why HCI Principles Matter

Good HCI design reduces cognitive load, prevents errors, and creates interfaces that users can navigate intuitively. This page demonstrates core Nielsen-Molich heuristics through practical, visible implementation.
